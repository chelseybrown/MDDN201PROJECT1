Wellington Internet Infrastructure

Wellington's infrastructure in terms of the internet is physically present power lines, cables, modums, etc, and very much present and hold a great deal of importance in every wellingtonians life

Power Lines

Power lines are made up of wood, cables, wires, and rubber, but when you combine those materials, step back and look at them as a whole, you see that their purpose is much, much more. There sole purpose is to provide power to crucial things we need in everyday life. Theyre fundamental to todays world and hold great value to every person not only in Wellington, but around the world.

Digital signs

The signs that show the wait time, estimated time, and schedule times of when buses will arrive at a bus stop are determined by gps located inside the bus to which it is connected to the signs in the hopes to create accurate times for patient passangers.

CCTV

Closed-circuit television, commonly known as CCTV, is widespread throughout almost all public areas, so much so that they have become an acceptable part of society. Used as record day to day footage of the public CCTV provides information to a wider networking infrastruction, thus providing us as individuals the same connection.
